Dominique Paschal Is the Co-founder , owner, CEO and Family Nurse Practitioner for Purposely Evolving. She started her career helping others after she obtained her Bachelors of Arts in Psychology with a minor in Deaf studies From the University of Iowa in 2008. She felt her heart was not satisfied and she did not reach her purpose so she decided to change courses and become a Registered Nurse. She started her career in nursing in 2008 as a certified nursing assistant. She graduated with her LPN Diploma in 2010 from Galen College of nursing. She knew this was the just beginning for her so she continued her education while being newly Married to Co-Founder Marcus Paschal and graduating With her Associates Degree in Nursing five months pregnant as Valedictorian from Galen College of nursing in 2010! She graduated with Honors and completed her APRN in Family Nurse Practice at the University of Tampa. She holds her Certification as a Family Nurse Practitioner at Tampa General where she has been practicing since graduation. Dominique has the desire to provide passionate, caring, intelligent, and comprehensive care to her patients to assist them in preventing or reversing diseases that are caused by obesity and other underlying conditions.


Dominique loves Music, comedy and reading novels! She loves to dance and sing! She is also a sports mom with three athletic children that keep her active and happy! Dominique is passionate, positive and loves to encourage others!



Marcus Paschal brings a wealth of knowledge with his long documented successful athletic career. A graduate of the Univ. of Iowa where he was one of the Top Safeties in the Country before enjoying a five year NFL career with the Philadelphia Eagles and Baltimore Ravens. He understands exactly what the body needs to be the best you and enjoy an Healthy lifestyle.

Marcus has been the Head Football Coach the past 8 years at his alma mater Largo High School, where he has helped over 50 scholar/athletes further their education and athletic careers Collegiately.
